- about us -

our aim

ThePlantBase.co was made to bring people together through the ideas of good food, community and creativity, all in the package of sustainability. We want to share our passion for healthy, high quality food with everyone and show its possible to be a thriving business whilst still taking care of the planet. We use recycled or upcycled materials for the renovation of our venues and are committed to utilising renewable energy sources for our day to day running as much as possible.

our menu

Our fully vegan menu offers a small but expertly prepared range of delicious options. With as much as possible being locally sourced, the food is something the whole community can be proud of. We strive to keep things fresh by regularly adding new options and updating our selection based on your suggestions and our own ideas.

your space

ThePlantBase© is your base. We want to offer and enviroment thats everyone can feel safe and comfortable in. Social areas for friends and family, quiet spaces for work and study, and a backroom for a range of events run by us and by you. We encourage people to showcase their passion and creativity at our open mic nights, and implore you to organise your own events. if you have an idea for an evening or string of evenings get in touch and make the space your own.

- Events -

food tasting

Our changing menu is one of the reasons people keep coming back, and Once a month we offer an evening for you to come and try our new creations. There's always a selection of new, and current menu items to try. feedback and suggestions are encouraged with the goal of giving people what they want, not just what we think they want.
Time: The evenings begin at 6:30 and typicaly last around two hours.
Price: £5 per person

open mic

do you sing? rap? tell jokes? ever wanted to do a ted talk but never got an invite? here's your opportunity to share it all. open mic nights give people a stage, and the beautiful audience they deserve. If you're a performer, or just want to see people perform, come down and get involved.
Time: Every friday, 7-10pm
Price: Free entry and half price drinks for performers.

poetry reading

Walt Whitman. Sylvia Plath. Maya Angelou. Edgar Allan Poe. Though the ages, poets have inspired people with their words, and at the plant base we belive in the power of these words, and want to share them with you. We reguarly put together an evening of people reading poems; some classics, some originals, some funny, some deeply moving. Come along and get lost in words with us.
Time: Second and last Tuesday of every month. 7-8:30pm.
Price: £5 per person.

your next event

The aim of The Plant Base is to give you a space to call your own, to put your stamp on, and what better way to do that than to host your own event. We have a lagre back room that is capable of being transformed into a multitude of spaces. We are equipped to have audio set up for music gigs, theatre performances and seminars. We have the facilities to provide cattering and a licence to server alcohol. The options and opportunities are plentiful so please get in contact with us, and share your vision with our community.
